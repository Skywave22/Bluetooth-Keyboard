## Full description (under 4000 chars)

```
Turn your Android phone into a wireless keyboard, mouse, media remote, presenter and fully custom gamepad — with nothing to install on the PC. BluePilot Remote uses Bluetooth HID, the same standard as real keyboards and mice, so it works with Windows, macOS, Linux, Android TV and most smart TVs out of the box.

REAL BLUETOOTH HID — NO PC SOFTWARE
Pair once and your phone IS a keyboard and mouse. No server programs, no Wi-Fi, no account, no cloud. Input travels directly over Bluetooth.

COMPLETE CONTROL SUITE
- Mouse: smooth trackpad with tap-to-click, right-click, scroll strip, adjustable sensitivity, smoothing and precision pen mode
- Keyboard: full PC keys — shortcuts (Copy/Paste/Undo), F1-F12, arrows, combos like Alt+Tab and Win+L, plus a text bar that types whole sentences
- Numpad, Multimedia remote and Presenter controls for work and play
- Gamepad: a real HID gamepad recognized by games, plus keyboard-fallback modes

BUILD YOUR OWN GAMEPAD
Design controllers on a blank canvas: place buttons, triggers, analog sticks and D-pads anywhere, resize them, pick shapes and colors, map each control to any gamepad button, tune stick dead zones. Save unlimited profiles (FPS, Racing, Fighting...), switch instantly, share them as files.

CUSTOM CONTROL LAYOUTS
Create your own remote screens from widgets: trackpads, buttons, sliders, joysticks, scroll strips, D-pads and swipe-gesture zones. Drag, resize and restyle everything — colors, opacity, corner radius, labels, icons. Split the screen into combo zones (trackpad + keys, corner pads and more). Import and export layouts as files.

MACROS AND GESTURES
Record real input sequences and replay them with one tap. Build macros step by step: key chords (Ctrl+Alt+Del), text typing, media keys, delays. Bind macros to any custom button or swipe gesture. Two-finger tap gestures supported.

MAKE IT YOURS — THEMES
8 built-in visual themes with live previews: Liquid Glass, Hawaii Night and Day, Cockpit HUD, Dark Neon, OLED Black, Minimal Light and classic Pilot Dark. The whole app re-skins instantly, dark and light.

PRIVACY FIRST
- No account, no ads, no analytics, no internet use
- Bluetooth permissions are used only to connect to your devices
- Optional secure-screen mode blocks screenshots
- Backups disabled: your layouts and macros stay on your phone

REQUIREMENTS
- Android 10 or newer
- A phone that supports Bluetooth HID Device mode (most modern phones do; the app detects and reports if yours does not)
- Any host that accepts Bluetooth keyboards/mice

Note: a small number of phone models/ROMs block Android's HID Device mode. BluePilot shows a clear message if your device is affected.
```
